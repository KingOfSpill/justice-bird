Test Procedure 01:

1) Open the game
2) Press the button on screen labelled "START"
3) Press the "ESC" Key on your Keyboard
4) Once the word "Pause" appears on screen and the bird stops moving, press the "ESC" key again
5) Pass if
	- The bird begins moving again
	- The word "Pause" dissapears from the screen
6) Press the "ESC" Key on your Keyboard
7) Once the word "Pause" appears on screen and the bird stops moving, press the button on screen labelled "Resume"
8) Pass if
	- The bird begins moving again
	- The word "Pause" dissapears from the screen
