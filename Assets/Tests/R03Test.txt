Test Procedure 03:

Test for not being able to poop without food
	1) Open the game
	2) Press the button on screen labelled "START"
	3) Press the "Spacebar" key on your Keyboard
	4) Pass if
		- No poop appears

Test for being able to poop with food
	1) Open the game
	2) Press the button on screen labelled "START"
	3) Using the arrow keys, move the bird to collide with the food, which should be floating and spin
	4) After colliding with a food object, press the "Spacebar" key on your Keyboard
	5) Pass if
		- Poop appears near the bird
